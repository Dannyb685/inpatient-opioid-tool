Advanced inpatient clinical decision support to mitigate high-risk opioid prescribing.

PrecisionAnalgesia by Lifeline Medical Technologies is a comprehensive clinical decision support tool engineered to integrate directly into inpatient clinical workflows. Developed to support opioid stewardship, this application provides vital calculations and clinical logic to enhance patient safety and reduce adverse events. Key Features: - Advanced Opioid Calculator: Accurately calculate daily MME and perform complex opioid conversions, including considerations for active metabolites (e.g., Morphine-6-glucuronide). - Renal & Hepatic Gating Logic: Built-in recommendations and dosage adjustments for patients with compromised renal or hepatic function. - Methadone Guardrails: Replace dangerous static conversion tables with advanced pharmacokinetic modeling that accounts for variable half-lives. - OUD Consult Assistant: A standardized workflow for Opioid Use Disorder assessments (incorporating COWS) to recommend evidence-based medication-assisted treatment (MAT) like Buprenorphine/Naloxone. - Pain Assessment Tracking: Detailed VAS scales, pain mapping, and symptom checklists to monitor treatment efficacy over time. Regulatory & Safe Harbor Notice: PrecisionAnalgesia is categorized as a Non-Device Clinical Decision Support (CDS) tool. It is intended to provide evidence-based information to healthcare professionals to assist them in managing patient care. This tool is designed to support, but does not replace, the independent clinical judgment of a qualified healthcare provider. The clinician is solely responsible for all final diagnosis and treatment decisions.
