The Problem & Solution
⚠ The Problem
  • 70-90% of drug safety alerts in EHR systems are overridden by clinicians — alert fatigue (van der Sijs et al., JAMIA 2006; Phansalkar et al., JAMIA 2013)
  • Doctors click past Boolean warnings — alert fatigue is endemic
  • MDCalc requires manual entry; low utilization at the point of care
  • Opioid-induced respiratory depression kills silently post-order
✦ The Solution
  • SMART on FHIR CDS Hook engine — zero manual data entry
  • Auto-pulls patient data; surfaces a specific, guideline-cited recommendation the clinician can accept or override (override logged)
  • 10-rule deterministic safety engine + OIRD risk scoring (PRODIGY), MME threshold logic, CDC naloxone co-prescribing prompts & pharmacogenomic guidance
  • <100ms response latency — specific, actionable guidance
