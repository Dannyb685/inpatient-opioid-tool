Glass Box — Show the Math

No machine learning. No black box. The engine applies these published rules, in this order:

  • Phase 1 (short-term): linear reduction — a fixed step of Start × rate% each week, until the dose reaches 30% of start.
  • Phase 2: exponential reduction — Current × (1 − rate%) per step, softening the landing.
  • Long-term mode: pure exponential reduction applied monthly (CDC slow-taper logic).
  • Minimum decrement: drops <1 MME alternate Hold / Force (−2 MME) to keep the taper moving.
  • Jump-off: below 10 MME, extend dosing interval; below 5 MME, discontinue.
  • Organ gates: conversion factors adjust — or hard-stop — for renal and hepatic impairment.
