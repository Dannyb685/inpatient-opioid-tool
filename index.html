Analgesia Tool

The next generation of Inpatient Opioid Management. A specialized Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) for high-acuity settings.

View iOS Prototype

Executive Summary

Analgesia Tool is designed to mitigate high-risk opioid prescribing errors and standardize complex OUD management workflows. By embedding clinical logic directly into the workflow, we provide a distinct, accountable, and high-value alternative to general calculators.

The Problem

Clinical errors and workflow inefficiencies in opioid management lead to preventable harm and waste precious clinician time.

Medication Errors

High risk of mathematical mistakes during opioid rotation, conversion (MME), and dose tapering.

Safety Gaps

Failure to consistently apply safety checks (e.g., renal/hepatic impairment) before prescribing.

Fragmented Workflows

Clinicians must use multiple scores, calculators, and external references to manage complex scenarios.

The Solution: 5-Tab Architecture

Integrated safety-conscious clinical aid.

1

Assessment

Patient Risk Stratification

  • • Inputs: Demographics, Organ Function
  • • Calculates: PRODIGY Risk Score
  • • Output: Sets global safety locks
2

Protocols

Action Engine

  • • Bernese Method (Micro-Induction)
  • • Standard Induction
  • • Withdrawal Management
3

MME Calculator

Dose Conversion & Rotation

  • • Total 24h MME Calculation
  • • Auto-reduction (default 30%)
  • • "Trauma Mode" Toggle
