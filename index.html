The "Opioid Precision" Engine

A proprietary Clinical Validation Engine that sits between the clinician and the prescription. It runs 50+ concurrent safety checks in milliseconds.

Renal Gating

Algorithm automatically detects GFR < 30 or Dialysis. Hard-blocks active metabolites (Morphine/Codeine) and recommends safe alternatives (Fentanyl/Hydromorphone).

Hepatic Shunt Logic

Identifies Child-Pugh Class C. Adjusts bioavailability calculations for Hydromorphone (4x increase in liver failure) to prevent overdose.

Bernese Protocol

Automated micro-induction scheduling for complex OUD patients, transforming a manual 7-day paper chart into a digital order set.
