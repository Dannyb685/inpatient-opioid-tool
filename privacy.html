Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

This policy applies to the PrecisionAnalgesia iOS application available on the Apple App Store, the Precision Analgesia web tools, and all associated clinical decision support products developed by Lifeline Medical Technologies, LLC.

1. No User Data Collection

Lifeline Medical Technologies, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. The PrecisionAnalgesia application and our associated clinical decision support tools do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal user data, patient health information (PHI), or analytics data. We do not use any third-party analytics services, tracking SDKs, or advertising frameworks.

2. "Glass Box" Architecture

Our applications operate completely on-device or within the secure confines of your institution's EHR. No data ever leaves your device or EHR environment to be processed on our servers. All clinical calculations and decision support logic execute locally.

3. Third-Party Services

The PrecisionAnalgesia application does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. No user data is shared with third parties for any purpose.

4. Changes to this Policy

If we update our privacy practices, we will reflect those changes on this page. However, our commitment to zero data collection remains foundational to our architecture.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: dan@lifelinemedtech.org
