Clinical Scope FAQ

Why is Methadone locked in the calculator?
Methadone exhibits non-linear kinetics and variable half-life. Standard equianalgesic ratios are dangerous for induction. Our engine locks calculation to force specialist consultation or use of the specific "Methadone Induction" module.
Why is Buprenorphine MME not displayed?
As a partial agonist with a ceiling effect, Buprenorphine does not follow linear MME logic. Assigning a static MME creates false equivalence. We treat it as a distinct class.
Data Retention Policy
Opioid Precision is a local processing engine. No patient health information (PHI) is transmitted to cloud servers. All calculations occur on-device. See Privacy Policy.
