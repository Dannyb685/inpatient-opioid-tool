# App Store Custom Product Pages (CPP) Metadata & Assets

**App:** Opioid Precision  
**Version:** 1.0.0  

This document defines the metadata and asset strategy for the three primary Custom Product Pages targeting distinct user intents. These pages are designed to improve conversion rates by applying specific keywords and visuals to separate deep links.

---

## Asset Specifications (Critical)

**Required Dimensions for iPhone (6.5" & 6.7" Displays):**

* **Portrait**: `1284 x 2778 pixels` OR `1242 x 2688 pixels`
* **Formats**: PNG (No transparency) or High-Quality JPEG.
* **Status Bar**: Must be cleaned (100% Battery, 9:41 AM, Full WiFi).
